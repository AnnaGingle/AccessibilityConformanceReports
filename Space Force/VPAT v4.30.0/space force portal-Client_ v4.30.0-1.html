Evaluation Methods Used Anchor link

The trusted tester process and ANDI were used to create this report. Covered in this testing is all pages in the client facing application: Login Page, MySpace, About Us, Orbit Blog, News, Settings, Search Results, USSF Documentation, Sites & Applications, and the Landing Pages. Also included are the page loading state, and 400 and 500 error pages. This testing does not include pages that are outside of the scope of our team, such as the CAC authentication process, which is handled by an external service. It also does not cover the content management system (CMS) which is covered in a separate report.

Terms Anchor link

The terms used in the Conformance Level information are defined as follows:
  • Supports: The functionality of the product has at least one method that meets the criterion without known defects or meets with equivalent facilitation.
  • Partially Supports: Some functionality of the product does not meet the criterion.
  • Does Not Support: The majority of product functionality does not meet the criterion.
  • Not Applicable: The criterion is not relevant to the product.
  • Not Evaluated: The product has not been evaluated against the criterion. This can be used only in WCAG 2.x Level AAA.
